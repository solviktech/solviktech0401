Service Localization

Dragon Umrah

A localized service model built around the practical realities of Umrah operations — focused on coordination quality, user experience, and reliable delivery.

Project Overview

Dragon Umrah was developed as a service concept specifically shaped by the unique operational and cultural context of Umrah-related travel and assistance. The project focused on building a service model that could deliver coordinated, trustworthy, and user-appropriate experiences within a market where local understanding is essential.

Project Details
Scope

Service model design, operational planning, user journey mapping, coordination logic, and localization strategy for the Umrah market context.

Challenge

Designing a service model that works reliably within the specific logistics, expectations, and cultural sensitivities of Umrah-related operations.

Approach

We focused first on understanding the actual user journey and operational realities before designing service components that would be executable and sustainable in practice.

Execution

Service flow design, coordination process documentation, operational guidelines, and initial delivery support.

Result

A structured and localized service model with clear coordination logic, ready for operational deployment and further expansion.
