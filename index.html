About SOLVIKTECH

SOLVIKTECH is a cross-border solutions company built on a simple principle: real execution beats strategy on paper. We bridge China and the Middle East — connecting supplier networks, coordinating projects, and building market presence with people who understand both sides.

Founded by professionals with backgrounds in financial engineering and international business, we bring structured thinking and local knowledge to every engagement. Whether you are sourcing a product, launching a project, or entering a new market — we help you move with clarity and confidence.
